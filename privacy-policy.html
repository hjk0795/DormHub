Privacy Notice

Dorm Hub collects and uses the minimum personal information necessary for off-campus dormitory operations.

1. Personal Information Collected

  • Email address provided through Google Sign-In
  • Name provided through Google Sign-In

2. Purpose of Use

The collected personal information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Individual notices and announcements
  • Inspection schedule notifications
  • Inquiry handling
  • Move-in and move-out management
  • Resident identity verification and room assignment matching

3. Retention and Use Period

Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary for dormitory operations and will be deleted when it is no longer needed or when the purpose has been fulfilled.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Personal information is not provided to third parties in principle. However, it may be disclosed if required by applicable laws and regulations.

5. Contact

If you have any questions regarding personal information processing, please contact:

  • Department: Public Welfare Team
  • Email: hjk0795@ks.ac.kr

6. Effective Date

This notice is effective as of March 26, 2026.
