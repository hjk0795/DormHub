Door Lock
Password Policy

Kyungsung Univ. Off-campus Dormitory

⚠️ Changing the door lock password without permission is prohibited.

↓ Scroll down ↓
🚫

Why Password Changes Are Restricted

Under dormitory regulations, staff may need to enter your room for emergency repairs, safety inspections, pest control, security checks, or outsourced maintenance — even when you are not present.

Article 10 ⑥ — Room inspections are conducted with prior notice while residents are present. However, in emergencies (urgent repairs, outsourced maintenance, security, pest control, safety inspections), staff may enter even when the resident is absent. The resident will be notified afterwards.
Ref: Rules for Residents of the One-Room Dormitory (원룸 기숙사 사생 규칙)

Since there is no master key, staff use the designated password to access rooms in these situations. If the password is changed without notice, emergency response becomes impossible. Therefore, changing the password without authorization is prohibited.

Must Change? Notify Immediately

If you must change the password for unavoidable reasons, you must immediately email the manager with your room number and new password.
