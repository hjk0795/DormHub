Door Lock
Password Policy

Kyungsung Univ. Off-campus Dormitory

⚠️ Changing the door lock password without permission is prohibited. The manager must always have access for safety.

Managed by Dormitory Office

1 The room door lock password is managed by the dormitory office for facility management and emergency safety purposes.

This ensures that staff can respond to emergencies, conduct safety inspections, and perform necessary maintenance.

1 / 4
🚫

Do NOT Change the Password

2 Students are prohibited from changing the password without authorization.

If the password is changed without notice, it prevents the manager from responding to emergencies and may result in disciplinary action.

2 / 4
📧

Must Change? Notify Immediately

3 If you must change the password for unavoidable reasons, you must immediately email the manager with your room number and new password.

Room Inspection Policy

Room inspections are conducted with prior notice while residents are present.

However, in emergency situations — urgent repairs, outsourced maintenance, security, pest control, or safety inspections — staff may enter even when the resident is absent. The resident will be notified afterwards.

This is why the manager must always have access. Changing the password without notice prevents emergency response.
